1-by-1 communication model

Very primitive math actions
[1] Set A to int16 number.
[2] Set B to int16 number.
[3] Get A*B as int32 number
[4] Get A + B and overflow flag as int16 numbers.
[5] Get A - B and overflow flag as int16 number.

[1] seta xxxx\n
[2] setb xxxx\n
[3] geta*b\n
[4] geta+b\n
[5] geta-b\n
